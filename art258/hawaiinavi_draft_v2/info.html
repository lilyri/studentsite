Spam Jam Festival

The Waikiki SPAM JAM is a street festival that celebrates the people of Hawaii's love for Spam, a canned meat from Hormel Foods.
This street gestival is great for all ages, as the event includes Hawaii's top resturants, two stages with free entertainment, and a variety of Hawaiian retailers.

Date: April 23, 2022
Location: Kalakaua Ave, Waikiki
Time: 4 pm at 8 pm
Cost: FREE
